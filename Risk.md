The correct answer is:

✅ **Technical risk**

### Explanation

The scenario says:

> A module depends on a **third-party payment gateway**, and during testing it is **not functioning properly due to a known bug in the vendor's code**.

The problem is caused by a **technical issue** (a software bug in an external dependency). This directly affects the system's functionality and integration.

Therefore, it is a **Technical Risk**.

---

### Why the other options are incorrect

#### ✅ Technical Risk (Correct)

Technical risks arise from problems related to:

* Software bugs
* Hardware failures
* Integration issues
* Third-party libraries or APIs
* Technology limitations

**Example:**

* A payment gateway API has a bug.
* A database crashes.
* An external library is incompatible with your application.

---

#### ❌ Business Risk

Business risks affect the organization's business goals, such as:

* Loss of customers
* Market competition
* Poor sales
* Budget or revenue issues

**Example:**

* Customers stop using the platform because a competitor offers lower prices.

---

#### ❌ Project Risk

Project risks affect the successful completion of the project, such as:

* Schedule delays
* Budget overruns
* Resource shortages
* Team members leaving

**Example:**

* A key developer resigns before project completion.

---

#### ❌ Legal Risk

Legal risks involve laws, regulations, contracts, or compliance.

**Example:**

* Violating data privacy regulations.
* Using copyrighted software without a license.
